Is Ozalyn really on "This Morning" or Dragons' Den?

A lot of people search for this — so here's the straight answer.

No. We found no credible evidence that Ozalyn (or Rozalyn) was featured on This Morning, backed on Dragons' Den, or endorsed by Peter Kay, Dr Hilary Jones, Jeremy Clarkson or any other named celebrity. Fake celebrity and TV endorsements are one of the most common tricks used to advertise diet supplements, and consumer bodies across Europe have warned about exactly this pattern. If you see an advert claiming a famous face is behind Ozalyn, treat it with caution — and judge the product on its ingredients and price instead.

Heads-up: "As seen on TV" and celebrity-endorsement claims for diet pills are frequently fabricated. Genuine information comes from the official store's own product page, not from look-alike news articles.
